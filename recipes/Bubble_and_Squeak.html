Bubble & Squeak
(from Leanne Brown's "Good and Cheap" cookbook p. 63)
ISBN:978-0-7611-8499-7

Description

"Bubble & Squeak is a traditional British weekend breakfast food
meant to use up the leftovers from the night before. Because it's
British, of course the recipe includes mashed potatoes! It's basically
a big potato pancake with stuff mixed into. Chop up any other stray
vegetables you have lying around.

"The adorable name comes from the sound this dish makes in the pan,
the squake coming from the cabbage, which is the most common mix-in
for the potato. The method [Leanne Brown] is giving you is for one big
pancake, but you could make smaller pancakes for a neater presentation.
You can also forget the pancakes entirely and make a hash out of the mixture."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup mashed or root vegetables
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Combine the mashed potatoes and root veggies in a large bowl.

  2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and mix everything together
    into a big, thick kind of dough.

  3. Melt the butter in a cast-iron or non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    Add the mixture to the pan and press it into a flat pancake.
    Let it cook 5 to 7 minutes without touching it.

  4. Using a spatula to check the underside of the pancake, it is
    time to flip if it's a golden-brown color!

  5. Let it cool for 10 minutes, then slice into wedges to serve.
